
# Decision Log Entries

| Decision Date | Status | Decision Summary | Alternatives Considered | Reasoning | Made By | Other Information |
|---------------|--------|------------------|-------------------------|-----------|---------|---------------|
| YYYY-MM-DD    |  Accepted or Rejected | A brief summary of the decision made | List of other approaches considered | A summary of why the decision was made or Description | Name or team | Link to the work item or additional documentation |

### Example Entry
| Decision Date | Decision | Alternatives Considered | Reasoning | Made By | Work Required |
|---------------|----------|-------------------------|-----------|---------|---------------|
| 2025-02-26    | Use Kubernetes for container orchestration | Docker Swarm, Mesos | Kubernetes offers better scalability and community support | DevOps Team | Update deployment scripts |
## How to Use

1. **Decision Date**: The date the decision was made.
2. **Decision**: A one-sentence summary of the decision.
3. **Alternatives Considered**: A list of other approaches considered.
4. **Reasoning**: A brief summary of why the decision was made.
5. **Made By**: The name or team who made the decision.
6. **Work Required**: A link to the work item or additional documentation.
