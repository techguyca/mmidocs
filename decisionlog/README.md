## How to Use

1. **Decision Number**
2. **Decision Date**: The date the decision was made.
3. **Decision**: A one-sentence summary of the decision.
4. **Description/Reasoning**: A brief summary of why the decision was made.
5. **Made By**: The name or team who made the decision.
6. **Decision Record or RFC where used or Other details**: A link to the work item or additional documentation.
